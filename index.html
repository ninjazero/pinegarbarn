THE BARN AT PINEGAR FARMS

WEDDINGS, KIDS EVENTS, BIRTHDAY PARTIES, CAMP-OUTS, REUNIONS, AND MORE...

Located in Loomis California

Host your next event at the Barn at Pinegar Farms. Located under 10 minutes from I-80 in Loomis this impressive small farm boasts spacious outdoor areas, exotic pool party atmosphere, and a large game room located inside the barn for games and activities. It is an amazing setting for kids birthday parties with plenty of room for inflatable bounce houses, outdoor entertainment, and room to run.

Amenities:
  • Swimming Pool
  • Trampoline
  • Open Grassy Area (2.5 acres in total) Lots of yard games.
  • Grape Vines and Fruit Trees including Satsuma Mandarins
  • Game Room (full size Billiards Table and other games)
  • Barn Space (upstairs and down)
  • Free Parking

Moon Glow Band

Make your special occasion more exciting with live music! Moon Glow is a quartet of professionals who are multi-instrumentalists and will feature sax, clarinet, flute, guitar, piano, bass, or drums. We are all vocalists. Our extensive repertoire of songs can be tailored to any event: Parties, weddings, birthdays or any holiday. Popular standards from the 1920’s through the 1980’s are our specialty. Special programs include; Patriotic, St. Patrick’s Day, Mardi Gras, Cinco DeMayo, Mothers and Fathers Day, Christmas/Holiday and New Year’s Eve. We specialize in Octoberfest music under the band name “Alpenmeisters.” Music lists take into consideration the age group of the audience and type of occasion.
